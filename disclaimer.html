About the Site

The contents of this site, including the assessment questions, were all found online through websites such as Quizlet and Quizizz. The actual lesson content was largely inspired by Mandy Rice's AP Psychology Videos on YouTube. This website merely acts as a convenient gathering place for the information.

The notes are formatted in a paraphrased way, meaning that they are, for the most part, worded concisely, but there will be examples and pictures.

*** The FRQs are taken directly from College Board's website.




Assessments

The unit assessments are 30 question tests that can be retaken as many times as possible (there will be different questions every time).

The final assessment is a 100 question test that uses questions from all the unit tests combined, and it can also be retaken as many times as needed (again, different questions each time).
